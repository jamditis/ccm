# 2026 Excellence in NJ Local News Awards
## Recommended Winners from Story Submissions Analysis

**Analysis Date:** January 2026
**Submissions Reviewed:** 1,472 stories from NJ News Commons members
**Methodology:** Six specialized subagents evaluated submissions across key categories, reading and analyzing full article content to assess quality, impact, and award-worthiness.

---

## Executive Summary

After comprehensive analysis of 1,472 story submissions, we identified **30 top candidates** across six award categories. Each story was evaluated based on:
- Journalistic quality and sourcing
- Community impact and relevance
- Investigative depth and originality
- Alignment with ELN award criteria (considering resources available to each outlet)

The strongest submissions demonstrate hyperlocal accountability journalism, environmental justice reporting, and coverage that amplifies underrepresented community voices.

## TOP AWARD RECOMMENDATIONS

### TIER 1: HIGHEST PRIORITY (Scores 9-10/10)

These stories represent exceptional journalism deserving of top recognition:

#### 1. **HudPost - "State's Star Witness Tied Fulop to Pay-to-Play Scheme"**
- **Score:** 10/10
- **Category:** Investigative / Hyperlocal Watchdog
- **URL:** https://hudpost.com/states-star-witness-tied-fulop-to-pay-to-play-scheme-ags-office-declined-to-pursue-case/
- **Why:** Obtained sealed court documents revealing Jersey City Mayor Fulop may have been involved in pay-to-play scheme. Documents $260,720 in city contracts flowing to cooperating witness's firm. Exposes selective prosecution by AG's office. Classic investigative journalism from a hyperlocal outlet.

#### 2. **Jersey Shore Online - "Whistleblower Suits Accuse Howell Officials Of Racial Profiling"**
- **Score:** 10/10
- **Category:** Hyperlocal Watchdog / Social Justice
- **URL:** https://www.jerseyshoreonline.com/howell/whistleblower-suits-accuse-howell-officials-of-racial-profiling-retaliation/
- **Reporter:** Stephanie A. Faughnan
- **Why:** Explosive documentation of alleged civil rights violations through court filings and text messages. Councilman's texts reveal monitoring recycling bins for "Modelo beer bottles" to identify undocumented residents. Shows two township officials resigned rather than implement discriminatory policies.

#### 3. **The New Jersey Democrat - "Paying George Norcross's 'Corruption Tax'"**
- **Score:** 9.5/10
- **Category:** Investigative Journalism
- **URL:** https://njdemocrat.substack.com/p/paying-george-norcrosss-corruption
- **Why:** Exposes systematic corruption through State Comptroller report. Documents $6 million annual overcharge to Toms River alone. Shows how political machine operates across party lines to extract "corruption tax" from taxpayers.

#### 4. **Montclair Local - "Everything We Know About Montclair Schools' Massive Deficit"**
- **Score:** 9.5/10
- **Category:** Education Journalism
- **URL:** https://montclairlocal.news/everything-we-know-now-about-montclair-schools-massive-deficit/
- **Why:** Sustained investigative reporting on $19.6 million school deficit. Uncovered 70 pages of unpaid invoices hidden from auditors. Documents systemic financial mismanagement affecting entire community.

#### 5. **Morristown Green - "NJ Sues Pfizer Over Water Contamination in Morris Plains"**
- **Score:** 9/10
- **Category:** Environmental & Public Health
- **URL:** https://morristowngreen.com/2025/10/03/nj-sues-pfizer-over-cancer-linked-water-contamination-in-morris-plains/
- **Why:** Connects decades of industrial pollution to current public health threats. TCE levels 6x higher than cleanup goals. Links to kidney cancer, lymphoma, and liver cancer. Excellent use of scientific data and regulatory history.

#### 6. **Jersey Shore Online - "Why Flooding On LBI Is Becoming The New Norm"**
- **Score:** 9/10
- **Category:** Environmental & Public Health
- **URL:** https://www.jerseyshoreonline.com/southern-ocean/why-flooding-on-lbi-is-becoming-the-new-norm/
- **Why:** Exemplary climate journalism making global warming locally relevant. Features NJ State Climatologist. Documents tidal flooding increase from <1 day/year (1950s) to 95-135 days/year by mid-century.

#### 7. **Reporte Hispano - "Hispanic Businesses and Economy at Risk Due to Trump's Immigration Policies"**
- **Score:** 9/10
- **Category:** Ethnic & Community Media
- **URL:** https://reportehispano.com (Spanish-language coverage)
- **Why:** True ethnic media serving Latino community. Combines data journalism ($194B NJ Latino GDP) with human stories. Documents 80% business sales drops. Reveals wage theft enabled by immigration fear.

#### 8. **Morristown Green - "Gun Safety Rally: Survivors and Students Demand Action"**
- **Score:** 9/10
- **Category:** Social Justice & Community Impact
- **URL:** https://morristowngreen.com/2025/06/08/advocates-rally-in-morristown-for-stronger-gun-safety-laws/

## METHODOLOGY NOTES

**Evaluation Process:**
1. Six specialized subagents reviewed 1,472 submissions
2. Each agent identified top candidates in their category
3. Full articles were fetched and read via WebFetch
4. Stories scored on 1-10 scale across multiple criteria
5. Cross-category comparison identified overall top performers

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Investigative depth and original reporting
- Community impact and relevance
- Source diversity and documentation
- Solutions-oriented coverage where applicable
- Consideration of outlet resources (per ELN judging philosophy)

**Limitations:**
- Some article URLs were inaccessible or behind paywalls
- Echo Press/Echo News TV articles had technical access issues
- Evaluation focused on individual stories, not series/projects
