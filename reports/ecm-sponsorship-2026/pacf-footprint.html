Our Discretionary Grantmaking Footprintprincetonareacf2024-11-05T09:39:51-05:00

Our fundholders recommend grants to nonprofits across the country. Our discretionary grants programs, including our Community Impact grants program and The Bunbury Fund, award grants locally.

The geographic region covered by the Princeton Area Community Foundation:

  • Mercer County
  • Eastern Bucks County, PA, including New Hope, Morrisville, Washington Crossing and Yardley
  • Northern Burlington County, including Bordentown and Bordentown Township
  • Southern Hunterdon County, including Lambertville, West Amwell, adjacent parts of East Amwell
  • Southern Middlesex County, including Plainsboro and Cranbury
  • Southern Somerset County, including Montgomery, Rocky Hill, and adjacent sections of Franklin Township
